隐私政策

Privacy Policy

欢迎使用 Power Renamer。我们非常重视您的隐私保护。本隐私政策说明了我们如何收集、使用和保护您的个人信息。

Welcome to Power Renamer. We take your privacy very seriously. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

信息收集

Information Collection

我们只收集应用程序运行所必需的最少信息：

We only collect the minimum information necessary for the application to function:

  • 文件名和路径信息（仅用于重命名操作，且仅在本地使用）
  • File names and path information (used only for renaming operations and processed locally)

此外，仅在您主动报告错误时，我们才会收集以下设备信息以帮助解决问题：

Additionally, only when you actively report an error, we collect the following device information to help resolve issues:

  • 设备系统版本
  • Device system version
  • 设备制造商
  • Device manufacturer
  • 设备型号
  • Device model

信息使用

Information Usage

我们收集的信息仅用于：

The information we collect is only used for:

  • 执行文件重命名操作
  • Performing file renaming operations
  • 在您主动报告时协助解决技术问题
  • Assisting with technical issues when you actively report them

信息安全

Information Security

我们采取适当的技术和组织措施来保护您的信息：

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • 所有操作都在本地完成，不会上传您的文件
  • All operations are performed locally, your files are never uploaded
  • 不收集任何文件内容
  • No file contents are collected
  • 不与第三方共享您的信息
  • Your information is not shared with third parties

政策更新

Policy Updates

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策将在本页面上发布，并在必要时通知您重要更改。

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted on this page, and you will be notified of significant changes when necessary.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the "About" page in the application.
